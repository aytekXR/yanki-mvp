Questions, answered plainly.

How is the GEO score calculated?

Mentions ÷ answers. If you appear in 24 of 40 responses, your score is 60%. It's intentionally primitive and fully published; weighting by position and sentiment is on the roadmap.

Which engines do you check?

Claude and OpenAI today, with Gemini and Perplexity in the panel as the rollout completes. Every response shows which engine and model produced it.

Do I need an account?

No. Paste a URL, get a link to your live analysis, and watch it run. Accounts, scheduling, and trends come later.

How long does an analysis take?

About 10 minutes for a full run. The page shows each pipeline step live — crawl, profile, prompts, engine panel, footprint check, score.

Can I see the raw answers?

Always. That's the point. Every response is stored and shown with the exact snippet where your brand matched — or a plain "not mentioned in this answer."
